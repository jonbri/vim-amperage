INTRODUCTION                                    *amperage*

Mappings to execute VimScript lines and macro's fast and repeatable

                                                *amperage-<leader> @*
<leader>@                      Execute VimScript line under cursor
                               Works with repeat.vim

                                                *amperage-<leader> q*
<leader>q                      Repeate macro in the "q" register
                               Works with repeat.vim
